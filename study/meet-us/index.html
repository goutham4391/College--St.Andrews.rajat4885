Meet us

We are here to help you discover St Andrews and support you throughout the application process. Many of our events are still run online, which allows you to access a much greater range of topics, and there are also options available for you to visit the town and University.

If you have any questions or are not sure the best event to attend, you can email us directly at admissions.events@st-andrews.ac.uk.

In St Andrews

We are excited to be welcoming visitors back to St Andrews. Find out more about options available for a walking tour and information session with Admissions.

Online

Join us for one of our information events where you can find out about different levels of study and specific courses we run. There are also sessions available for parents and college counsellors.

In your country

Students who are thinking of applying to the University for undergraduate or foundation programmes can come to an undergraduate online visiting day to learn more about life at St Andrews and how to apply.

Ask a student

If you are interested in learning what it's like to be a student at St Andrews, you can speak to one of our student ambassadors. They'll let you know about their top tips, best study spots, favourite traditions and more.
