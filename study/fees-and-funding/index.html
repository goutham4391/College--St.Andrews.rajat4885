Fees and funding

Your tuition fees will depend on your course of study and your home domicile if you are an undergraduate student, or your chosen programme if you are a postgraduate student. You will also need to consider the costs of living, such as accommodation, food and travel.

There are a number of scholarships and support packages available at all levels of study, and the University can provide financial assistance and advice where necessary. A video guide has been created to help you with your scholarship application and also includes details on creating your funding financial profile.

Undergraduate fees and funding

The University of St Andrews offers a number of scholarships and support packages to undergraduate students each year, and there may also be other funding options available.

Postgraduate fees and funding

Discover specific information on the various fees for which you may be liable as a postgraduate student at the University of St Andrews, including tuition fees, as well as the various funding options that may be available to you.
